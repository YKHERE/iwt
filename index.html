ABOUT G.P College Nainital

Government Polytechnic College, Nainital, established in 1957, recently celebrated it’s 68th anniversary of imparting high quality education to the students of Uttrakhand. Every year an entrance exam is conducted by U.B.T.E.R Here at G.P Nainital, we reinvent the way we teach, so that our students not only keeps up with the modern times but also have the skills to bring about a change.
The Goverment Polytechnic Nainital is One of the best technical college in Uttarakhand, where Uttarakhand's top Faculties are deployed for teaching highest level theory and practical (both) classes from scrath and step by step.
