Provides essential knowledge of pharmacy practices, drug formulation, pharmacology, and healthcare. Prepares students to work as pharmacists in various sectors.

Govt Polytechnic Nainital

Established in 1975, Government Polytechnic Nainital offers diploma courses in engineering, IT, pharmacy, and office management. It is affiliated with UBTER and approved by AICTE. Admissions are through JEEP. The campus has hostels, labs, and placement support.
