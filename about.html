About G.P Nainital

Government Polytechnic Nainital was established in 1957, making it one of the oldest technical institutes in Uttarakhand. G.P. Nainital is widely recognized as one of the best institutions for technical education in the state. The college offers a variety of diploma programs catering to multiple engineering and technical fields. Its curriculum is thoughtfully designed to bridge the gap between theoretical academics and hands-on industry skills. The institute emphasizes both classroom learning and practical training to prepare students for real-world challenges. G.P. Nainital boasts a strong faculty team comprising experienced educators and industry professionals. Industry experts frequently collaborate with the college to provide students with up-to-date knowledge and training. The college maintains a vast and active alumni network spread across top industries in India and abroad. Students benefit from mentorship, internships, and placement support through this network. Government Polytechnic Nainital continues to empower future engineers and technocrats with quality technical education.
Government Polytechnic Nainital (known as Nainital Polytechnic) is a technical education institute located at Nainital, Uttarakhand. Government Polytechnic Nainital is a well-known technical institute located in the scenic hill town of Nainital, Uttarakhand. Established in 1975, the college has been providing quality diploma education in various technical fields for several decades. It is affiliated with the Uttarakhand Board of Technical Education, Roorkee (UBTER), and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The institute offers diploma courses in Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, and Electronics Engineering, as well as Information Technology, Pharmacy, and Modern Office Management. These programs are designed to equip students with practical and theoretical knowledge, preparing them for various industries. Government Polytechnic Nainital is known for its good infrastructure, including well-equipped labs, a large library, hostels, and sports facilities. The campus provides a productive learning environment supported by experienced faculty. Admission to the courses is done through the state-level Joint Entrance Examination for Polytechnics (JEEP). With its commitment to skill development and technical excellence, Government Polytechnic Nainital continues to be a preferred choice for students seeking diploma education in Uttarakhand.
